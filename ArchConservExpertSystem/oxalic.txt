Oxalic Acid

Solution: Aqueous solution of 5% Oxalic Acid

Application: The solution is applied on the encrustation 
for a few minutes each time .The chemical gradually 
dissolve the encrustation which is then washed of with 
distilled water. The process is repeated until most of 
the encrustation has been removed.

This technique is suitable for fragile objects and 
objects which have been almost completely mineralized. 
The chemical could cause problems to objects with considerable 
amounts of surviving metal. Oxalic acid should be handled with 
care because it is a deadly poison
