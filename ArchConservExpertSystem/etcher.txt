
Electric Etchers-Engravers 

These pneumatic tools operate with a reciprocal 
motion of a conical stylus,the force applied by the stylus,
and the number of cycles per minute can be adjusted
to the needs of each particular task. they are better 
suited for fragile or delicate objects but are also useful 
require a gentler touch for cleaning details on stronger objects. 
The etcher usually comes with several interchangible stylus for
varius types of debry. Although the speed is adjustable these 
tools always cause some small vibrations and it is better to 
avoid using them for very fragile objects
