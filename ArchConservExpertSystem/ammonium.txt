Ammonium Acetate

Solution: Aqueous solution of 5% Ammonium Acetate

Application: The solution is applied on the encrustation for 
a few minutes each time .The chemical gradually dissolve the 
encrustation which is then washed of with distilled water. 
The process is repeated until most of the encrustation has 
been removed.

This technique is suitable for fragile objects and objects 
which have been almost completely mineralized. The chemical 
could cause problems to objects with considerable amounts of 
surviving metal.
